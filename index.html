About

I like straightforward tools, legible systems, and websites that do not fight their visitors. Most of my work starts with code, but the interesting part is usually the shape of the problem: what deserves complexity, what should stay boring, and what should be removed entirely.

This homepage stays intentionally light: plain HTML, plain CSS, and no build step. Fast to load, easy to edit, and hard to break.
